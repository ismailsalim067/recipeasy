Recipe Name: Spaghetti Bolognaise

Description: A simple pasta dish

Cuisine: Italian

Difficulty: Easy

Cooking Time (minutes): 30

Author: user123

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients

  • 500g Mince
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 1 onion
  • 500g pasta
  • A handful of cheese

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion and fry it
  2. Add mince when onion is soft
  3. Start boiling pasta water
  4. Add tomatoes and stock to mince
  5. Serve vtogether with cheese
